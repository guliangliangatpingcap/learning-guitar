## 和弦计算

| Major Triad | 音名          | 简谱记号      |
| ----------- | ------------- | ------------- |
| C           | C - E - F     | 1 - 3 - 5     |
| C#          | C# - E# - F#  | #1 - #3 - #5  |
| Db          | Db - F - Ab   | b2 - 4 - b6   |
| D           | D - F# - A    | 2 - #4 - 6    |
| D#          | D# - F## - A# | #2 - ##4 - #6 |
| Eb          | Eb - G - Bb   | b3 - 5 - b7   |
| E           | E - G# - B    | 3 - #5 - 7    |
| F           | F - A - C     | 4 - 6 - 1     |
| F#          | F# - A# - C#  | #4 - #6 - #1  |
| Gb          | Gb - Bb - Db  | b5 - b7 - b2  |
| G           | G - B - D     | 5 - 7 - 2     |
| G#          | G# - B# -D#   | #5 - #7 -  #2 |
| Ab          | Ab - C - Eb   | b7 - 1 - b3   |
| A           | A - C# - E    | 6 - #1 - 3    |
| A#          | A# - C## - E# | #6 - ##1 - #3 |
| Bb          | Bb - D - F    | b7 - 2 - 4    |
| B           | B - D# - F#   | 7 - #2 - #4   |
